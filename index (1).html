About The Studio

Where Heritage Meets Contemporary Vision

At Etege Design, we believe that modern luxury should never forget its roots. Since 2015, our founders Rahel and Yemisirach have led a multidisciplinary team of 11–50 architects, interior specialists, and construction planners in reimagining what Ethiopian design can mean on the world stage.

We are renowned for integrating traditional Tibeb (ጥበብ) weaving motifs and geometric Fidel (ፊደል) scripts directly into structural modern home layouts — a design language that is unmistakably Ethiopian and uncompromisingly contemporary.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know before starting your project with Etege.

How long does a full interior project typically take?

▼

Residential projects generally range from 8–16 weeks depending on scope and material selection. Commercial buildouts may extend to 20–24 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

What is your pricing structure?

▼

We work on a milestone-based payment structure: 40% upon agreement, 30% at material procurement, and 30% upon final delivery. Custom furniture and build-only projects are quoted individually after consultation.

Do you work outside Addis Ababa?

▼

Currently we focus on projects within Addis Ababa and immediate surrounding areas. Regional and international projects are considered on a case-by-case basis for select clients.

Can I purchase individual pieces from the Tibeb or Fidel collections?

▼

Yes. While many clients commission full collection suites, individual pieces — from a single Fidel chair to a Tibeb-patterned accent wall — are available. Visit our showroom to view current stock and place custom orders.

Do you offer design-only services, or is build-out always included?

▼

We offer both. Many clients prefer our turnkey Design-Build package for seamless execution, but Design-Only and Build-Only engagements are available for architects, contractors, and homeowners who already have partners in place.
